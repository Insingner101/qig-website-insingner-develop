Our Vision

To establish BITS Pilani as one of the leading private institutions in Quantum Technologies and Information processing.

Our Mission

Establish one of the first research centers in a private university with state-of-the-art facilities for technology development and manpower training in quantum technologies in condensed matter and optics, as well as collaborate in developments in theory and algorithms

About US

Our Objectives

Research

Provide basic infrastructure for advanced materials development to put BITS Pilani on the map of forefront quantum information processing technologies.

Set up proof-of-principle experiments in optics and material science.

Education

Train undergraduate students from BITS and outside in the language and techniques of modern quantum technologies and information processing.

Attract BITS undergraduates to internship and research projects in BITS itself instead of looking outside.

Outreach

Foster collaboration among departments and campuses in research and development in quantum technologies.

Attract further investment in terms of funding and intellectual capacity from industry,governments and other universities both within India and abroad.
